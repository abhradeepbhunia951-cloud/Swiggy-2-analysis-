Customer Sales Analysis Projects

Objective:
To analyze customer and delivery data to understand delivery trends, top delivery, and  customer behaviour, region behaviour.

Dataset:
- Swiggy.csv

Tools used:
- Python(Pandas)
- Excel
- MySQL
Key Insights:

*"Cities with faster delivery times generally show higher ratings."
*"Certain areas consistently have high delivery times and low ratings, indicating operational issues."
*"City "Kolkata" performs best in both delivery speed and satisfaction."
*"Delivery with higher delivery speed gets more orders and better ratings as compared to delivery with low speed."


Dashboard:
The Dashboard shows total revenue, total orders, monthly delivery trends, top delivery, and city-wise delivery ratings performances.
